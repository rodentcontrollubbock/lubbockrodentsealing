About Our Content

All articles on this site are written to be useful for any Lubbock homeowner regardless of which service provider they eventually choose. We aim to be specific, practical, and honest about what rodent control actually involves — including the parts that are uncomfortable to talk about, like contamination cleanup and the limits of DIY methods.

If you find errors, outdated information, or have suggestions for topics we should cover, we appreciate the feedback. The goal is to make this resource as useful as possible for the homeowners who need it.
